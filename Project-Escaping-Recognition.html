Escaping Recognition

2025

This project investigates the layered systems of vision. Human and non-human lenses that shape how we are seen and understood. It examines the omnipresence of surveillance and perception revealing a world constantly interpreting us.

There are many situations in which one may wish to escape recognition entirely. Through the reimagination of anxieties, this project explores how perception is a negotiation and through adaptation, disruption and avoidance can become strategies for agency. While total disappearance is paradoxical, through understanding first to evade later we can be part of an informed negotiation with the act of being seen.

Drawing upon the notion of non-human intelligence - human, technological, animal - to investigate methods of evading them. I seek new methods for becoming unrecognizable in environments where I feel exposed. And in order to become comfortable in our habits, we first should be made uncomfortable by the ways we are surveilled.
